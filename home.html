Pablo de la Fuente is currently pursuing his MSc in Mathematics at the University of Victoria, under the supervision of Prof. Gourab Ray. He previously obtained his BSc in Mathematics, with honours, at UNAM under the supervision of Prof. Laura Eslava. As a mathematician, his main interests lie somewhere between probability and combinatorics. He also enjoys reading and writing poetry, and photography.
