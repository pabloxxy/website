Simulations

I enjoy simulating random objects. There is an almost mystical sense to it. In many of the cases, you are going to be (almost surely or at least with overwhelming probability) the first and only person in history to witness that particular sampled object.

This are coded in JavaScript specifically to work on web browsers. The codes are not perfect but they are made with love. I also tried to explain some things about the objects being simulated and/or the methods used.

  • Erdos Renyi graph with monotone coupling
  • Brownian Bridge to Brownian excursion via Vervaat transform
  • Brownian Motion and its Local Time Surface
